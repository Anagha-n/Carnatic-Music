Carnatic Music

Carnatic music is also known as the classical music of southern India.
For many years scientists were and still are trying to figure out what exactly is the science behind it. and what makes it so special...
Down below is some information I gathered from various sources.(Not all of them were Indian) I tried joining the pecies which were given to me to solve this gigantic puzzle to help someone with the same doubt as me.

A very special Song

Maithreem Bhajatha, a ragamalika (many ragas in the same song) was the first song to ge sung by any Indian by M.S Subbulakshmi in the first time any Indian sang in the United Nation. This song has a special importance in the history of India.

Famous for Carnatic music

M. S. Subbulakshmi

Madurai Shanmukhavadivu Subbulakshmi, fondly known as MS Subbulakshmi, was an Indian Carnatic singer from Madurai, Tamil Nadu. She was the first musician to be awarded the Bharat Ratna.

Sikkil Gurucharan

Sikkil C. Gurucharan is among the foremost young performing musicians of Carnatic music in India today. He won his first award in 1994 and was conferred many more after that.

T. M. Krishna

Thodur Madabusi Krishna who is also known as TMK is an Indian Carnatic vocalist, writer, activist, author and has won the Ramon Magsaysay award.

T. H. Vinayakram

Umayalpuram Sivaraman is a great indian ghatam player. Fondly also known as vikku vinayakram. He plays with a lot of energy. Do listen to him with the button below.

Sudha Ragunathan

Sudha Ragunathan is a Carnatic vocalist, singer and composer. She was conferred the Kalaimamani award the Padma Shri and Padma Bhushan.

M Balamurali

Dr. Mangalampalli Balamuralikrishna was a Carnatic music vocalist, musician, multi-instrumentalist, playback singer, and an amazing composer.

K. J. Yesudas

Kattassery Joseph Yesudas is an Indian playback singer and musician who sings Indian classical, devotional and film songs.

Umayalpuram K. Sivaraman

Umayalpuram Sivaraman is a great indian mridamgamist. He is one of the oldest players. He has also learned to play the violin but speacilises in mridamgam
