The science behind carnatic music

Carnatic Music is categorised into raagas. Venkatamakhi was the inventor of the melakartha system that is followed today. The melakartha system is the classification of ragas by their swaras.These ragas are classified by their swaras. The swaras form the backbone of the raaga. But just putting together words is not enough to make music. The swaras need to be sung in a specific frequency to be appealing to our ears.
