Music and Emotions

I had conducted an experiment, to check if the brain can sense whether the music is happy or sad. According to my research, the results were, everyone could sense if the music was sad or happy. while the sand music was played, time seemed to flow by slowly. While the upbeat song played... they wanted to move along with the beats. Within a minute everyone had the songs emotion inside them.
